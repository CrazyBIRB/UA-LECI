About Us

Founded in 1975 by Jonhy Greatguy, Gr8 Inc. is one of the leading employers in the Networking Paradise Village area, bringing quality products and reliable services for the local community and beyond.

Today, Gr8 Inc. employs over 3000 people across multiple sectors of production, winning "Local Company of the Year" various times alongside various commendations from independant organizations nationwide.

Our Mission

Ever since our founding, back when the company was but a humble startup, the purpose of Gr8 Inc. was always to provide the community of Networking Paradise Village and beyond the greatest services possible, and to launch the region into the future with the use of the most cutting edge tech.

To this day, every person working at the Gr8 Inc. remains commited to this goal, and works to achieve it every day.
